Max Temperature

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator.

More interesting, however, is that the fact the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was gathered.

The southern-most city is at a southern latitude of 54 degrees. Data ends at that latitude. Also there are no readings for cities located in the Arctic and Antarctic.

The University of Southern California is in a temperate zone, close to the Tropic of Cancer. The mild climate on the campus is because of this latitude and close proximity to the Pacific Ocean.
